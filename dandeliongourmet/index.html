Our Story

About Us

Dandelion Gourmet is a proudly Filipino food and beverage company committed to bringing people together through exceptional dining and entertainment experiences.

Our family of brands includes Ramen Young Wasabi, a Japanese-Filipino inspired restaurant known for its signature ramen and flavorful dishes, and two family-friendly karaoke destinations, Fajardo's Family KTV and Chorus Family KTV, each offering private KTV rooms, delicious food, and a fun atmosphere for all occasions.

Today, Ramen Young Wasabi proudly serves guests across three branches in Dasmariñas, Bacoor (Main Square Molino), and Las Piñas, Fajardo's Family KTV welcomes families and friends at its Dasmariñas branch, and Chorus Family KTV welcomes guests at its Robinsons Las Piñas branch.

At Dandelion Gourmet, our mission is simple: to create memorable moments by combining great food, warm hospitality, and enjoyable experiences for every guest.
